**NoCap** (No Cap on Fakes) is a complete end-to-end deepfake video detection system designed to address the challenge of hyper-realistic synthetic media. By combining state-of-the-art deep learning, visual explainability, and accessible web deployment, NoCap aims to protect public trust and personal privacy.

Live Web Application: [nocap-deepfake.streamlit.app](https://nocap-deepfake.streamlit.app/)

---

## ⚠️ Disclaimer
> **Important Note:** This tool is an academic research prototype. The underlying model has been trained and optimized specifically on the **DFDC (DeepFake Detection Challenge)** and **Celeb-DF v2** datasets. Consequently, it may show reduced detection rates or unexpected results when evaluated against video content generated by newer architectures (such as advanced diffusion models) or content that has undergone heavy social media compression. Results should be treated as diagnostic indicators rather than definitive legal or forensic proof.

---

## 🔴 The Problem
Deepfake technology, powered by generative adversarial networks and deep learning, produces synthetic media that is increasingly difficult to distinguish from authentic content. This poses critical threats to public trust, journalistic integrity, electoral processes, and personal privacy. NoCap addresses this gap by providing an accessible, explainable tool that requires zero installation.

## 🧠 System Architecture & Pipeline

The inference pipeline extracts data from uploaded videos and produces a graded, evidence-backed output:

1. **Frame Extraction:** OpenCV extracts 20 evenly spaced frames from uploaded videos.
2. **Face Detection:** Detects and crops faces using MTCNN.
3. **Feature Extraction & Scoring:** An EfficientNet-B4 compound-scaled convolutional neural network scores each face.
4. **Score Normalization:** Applies a calibrated dual-condition threshold for a final verdict.
5. **Explainability (Grad-CAM):** Grad-CAM visualisation highlights suspicious facial regions.
6. **Forensic Report:** A 5-level risk meter and downloadable PDF forensic report provide graded, evidence-backed outputs.

## 📊 Methodology & Training Protocols

NoCap utilizes a two-stage training approach to handle domain shift between training and real-world video:
* **Stage 1 (Pretraining):** Initial training on the large DeepFake Detection Challenge (DFDC) benchmark.
* **Stage 2 (Domain Adaptation):** Domain-adaptive fine-tuning on the Celeb-DF v2 benchmark.

### Performance Results (Celeb-DF v2 Test Split)
The final model surpasses all published baseline methods on that benchmark.

| Metric | Score | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Fake Detection Rate** | 97.35% | 97.35% of all actual fake videos correctly caught |
